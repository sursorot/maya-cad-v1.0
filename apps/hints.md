1. If you are working on walls -

    1.1. If you do not need Orthogonal walls, you can turn off the Ortho toggle in the settings panel (show icon or shortcut).
    1.2. You can move the midpoint of a wall to create an arched wall - two headed arrow.
    1.3. click on a wall, wait for the four header cursor to move the wall's orientation.
    1.4. If Ortho is ON, you can only move the wall horizontally or vertically depending on its intital orientation.
    1.5. You can rotate the entire selected wall through the circle on top.
    1.6. You can change the style of the walls through the Style Panel.
    1.7. Turn ON the centerline toggle to see the centerline of the wall. 
    1.8. You can place wall openings (doors, windows by clicking the opening tool)
    1.9. You can draw one, multiple-chained walls or rectangular rooms by changing the 'Drawing Mode' in the settings panel.
    1.10. You can choose the alignment of the centerline of the wall while creating it. 
    1.11. You can change the centerline alignment of a wall after creating it, but it will also change the overall dimensions of the wall and other walls linked with it.
